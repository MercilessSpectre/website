Details

In Revival of Azh'Rak, you play as a powerful wizard, one of the higher beings on the continent, as you try to help different civilizations flourish with your magic. Travel around different ruins to recall forgotten spells, and become a hero of the people in this high fantasy world.

Features

While there are multitudes of features not found in other games, we decided to list a few that are truly special.

  • No specific storyline. In order to complete the game, all you have to do is to restore civilization on more than half the continent. There is no linear progression in the game.
  • There is a complex spell hierarchy, but spells are not lerned from the skill tree. The player has to travel around the continent to remember certain spells.
  • And, lastly, all games come with our friendly customer support from our staff.
