Hi. We are PolyWizardry Entertainment.

PolyWizardry - Polygamous Wizards on Drugs!

Originally, our Founder and C.E.O (E.) had an ambitious idea of trying to create his own game design company. He posted on a few forums and with patience he found many people who were supportive and willing to help him. These people became his first staff members. Over time, our company's mission has shifted from simply succeeding in creating a game design company to trying to bring life and innovation into the video game industry.

We are currently developing our first game with fantastically crafted 3D models, within a moving story with the help of the Unreal Engine. You can view our progress in our Blog. As result of E's outstanding recruitment effort, we have many members around the globe in countries such as Canada, United Kingdom, Switzerland, United States of America, and Ukraine. It was once just a single person's dream, but now it is everyone's mission at PolyWizardry!

Revival of Azh'Rak

A 3D singleplayer RPG about restoring civilization to a forgotten continent.

In Revival of Azh'Rak, you play a powerful wizard, one of the higher beings on the continent, as you try to help different civilizations flourish with your magic. Travel around different ruins to recall forgotten spells, and become a hero of the people in this high fantasy world.
